RAINN

This project was initiated as part of my UI Design 1 class, where I was tasked with redesigning an existing website.

I chose to focus on the non-profit RAINN (Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network), as it aligned with my passion for supporting marginalized communities.

I aimed to enhance the user experience by improving visual hierarchy and reducing cognitive overload, creating a welcoming space for survivors seeking help.
